# PRD: HIM - Her Intelligent Mate

## 1. Executive Summary
HIM (Her Intelligent Mate) is an offline-first, AI-powered period companion designed to provide emotional, physical, and mental wellness support throughout the menstrual cycle. Unlike traditional trackers, HIM focuses on the "Emotional Context" of the user, offering empathetic AI chat and phase-specific wellness curation.

## 2. Core Value Propositions
- **Empathetic AI (USP)**: A context-aware assistant that understands the user's current cycle phase and mood.
- **Voice Support**: Hands-free interaction via ElevenLabs for comfort during high-stress periods.
- **Privacy First**: Offline-first architecture with local data persistence (WatermelonDB).
- **Gamified Wellness**: Building healthy habits through challenges, badges, and streaks.

## 3. Targeted User Personas
- Women seeking a more "human" and less "clinical" tracking experience.
- Users who experience significant mood shifts or PMS and need emotional support.
- Privacy-conscious individuals who prefer local data storage.

## 4. Key Features (Phase 1)
- **Cycle Engine**: Accurate prediction of Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulation, and Luteal phases.
- **AI Chat & Voice**: Conversational ally for health advice and emotional venting.
- **Mood Journaling**: Daily tracking of symptoms and emotional states.
- **Wellness Hub**: Curated library of articles, yoga videos, and lofi music.
- **Gamification**: Badges for streaks and mini-games for stress relief.
- **Partner Mode**: Secure sharing of cycle status with a partner.

## 5. Roadmap
- **Phase 2**: Smart Doctor Reports (PDF Export).
- **Phase 3**: Anonymous Community Forum.
- **Phase 4**: Advanced cycle syncing with wearable devices.
